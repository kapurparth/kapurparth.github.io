Project Title

Emotion-Aware Hindi-English Speech Translation

Project Description

This project aims to develop a speech translation system that captures emotional context when translating spoken Hindi to English text. By integrating emotion recognition capabilities into the translation pipeline, we seek to preserve the emotional nuances and contextual meaning that are often lost in traditional speech translation systems. The research focuses on developing models that can simultaneously process linguistic content and emotional prosody to produce more accurate and contextually appropriate translations.
